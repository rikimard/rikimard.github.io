Improving Scafol Landing Page

Overview

Scafol is a platform for monitoring, reporting, and managing construction projects for owners and contractors. The target audience is construction project owners, Real estate developers, and contractors.


My Role in the project

I was on the design team as a UI UX designer


The challenge

How might we increase the audience to start to sign up by giving them a call to action button to get more users to use the platform?


The old design

The New design

The most significant difference between the old and new designsc (except for the color and layout) is that visitors are still guessing what the product looks like. Even though there is a tagline and sub tagline there, it is not enough to make visitors clear about what exactly this website is. So I decided to put screenshots of our products to let the visitors know and clarify what we offer them.

The Result

Finally after improving the design, we got around 5% users to sign up per week.
