Giwang Sumsel - Explore your South Sumatera

What is Giwang Sumsel?

Giwang is an app to exploring a lot of potential local destinations in South Sumatera.


My Role in the Project

I worked on this project as a UX/UI Designer. Main areas of responsibility:
- UX design ( FLow & User Persona, wireframing, and prototyping)
- Front end Development (Website)


The Client

South Sumatra Department of tourism and culture

Project Date

December 2020 - March 2021

Project Summary

Giwang Sumsel app is a medium for disseminating information related to the potential of culture and tourism in South Sumatra. The app, primarily was designed to focus on how so that all of Indonesia or even the world can see the potential of culture and tourism in South Sumatra


The Challenge

How can we promote South Sumatra tourism and cultural information for travelers with a simple and easy-to-use mobile app?

Process

Information Architecture

Before making a wireframe, I created an information architecture first. Because with IA, I can easily comprehend, navigate, and scalable the app.

Wireframe

Mockup

I designed simple navigation with unique icons so that users can move from one page to another page quickly. We also provide information to travellers about what they can do, what the destination looks like, and how to get there.

Test of the Reach

Before and after launching the Giwang app, I tried to search "giwang sumsel" on Google, and we had a massive response from the media. Of course, there are a lot of the same apps like Giwang that provide information about South Sumatera tourism, but those apps or media usually come out from South Sumatera. So we hope this will be one of the significant innovations for my client to promote the local potential in South Sumatra.

Test Result

Giwang Sumsel has downloaded more than 1k downloads on the play store for almost six months after its release. In addition, Giwang Sumsel also became the best innovation in the OPD Government of South Sumatra Province in 2021.
