SCAFOL Web/Mobile

Scafol is B2B start-up that has a vision to help the construction sector, especially owners and contractors, to manage construction projects. We hope the project becomes easier to manage, more efficient, and in real time.


-

Today, there are more than 2k registered users and hundreds of active users per month. Most of the scaffolding users are medium contractors, project owners, and property developers. As part of scafol, my responsibility is not only to design the experience of scafol web and mobile. But it becomes a sweet spot between users, business and technical teams.

In my latest work, I designed a new interface for scafol mobile. There are some features we added in the new mobile version, such as dashboard, progress, finance, logistics, and forum. I also add new design icons and new design styles with a focus on simple navigation from one menu to another menu.
